West Point MS MATHCOUNTS

West Point Middle School is seeking members for a newly formed National Math Club and MATHCOUNTS Competition Team.

This is for children who enjoy math and desire more opportunities to:

  1. have fun doing mathematics,
  2. explore more math topics than typically taught in school,
  3. attempt very challenging problems, and
  4. meet other students with similar interests.

We will meet Tuesdays and Thursdays, 2:30-3:00PM, in room 126, on school days without an early release.

No transportation provided; parents of bus riders will need to pick up their children.
